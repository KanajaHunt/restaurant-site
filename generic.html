About Us

Phantom's humble beginning started in their dorm room. Tired of always relying on fast food, one day chef started dedicated his time to make at least homemade meal during the week.

This was just the start. Knowing that college students could use some homemade meals Chef began serving plates from their dorm room.

Fast forward today, Phantom is s successful booming restaurant in downtown.
